The Deal

How It
Works

01

Show Up

Arrive at Foxhole by 6:15 PM. Register once per season — free to join. All paces and fitness levels are welcome, no experience needed.

02

Run 5K

Group photo at 6:25, then we're off at 6:30. Pick a suggested route or create your own. About 3.1 miles through Shorewood's neighborhoods.

03

Stay for Drinks

Head back to Foxhole around 7:00 PM. Cold beer, good food, great people. This is the best part.

Wednesday Night Schedule

Get Moving

The
Routes

Scenic

Lake Loop

Runs along the Lake Michigan shoreline. Flat, beautiful views, especially at golden hour.

Neighborhood

North Route

Heads north on the Oak Leaf Trail through Shorewood's tree-lined streets. A local classic.

These are just suggestions — feel free to mix it up or create your own route. We all end up at the same place.
