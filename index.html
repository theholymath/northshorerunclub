North Shore Pub Run

Join the North Shore Running Community!

A running community for all levels of runners! Based in Shorewood open to all!

Every Wednesday Run starts at 6:30 PM!

Meet at the Foxhole Craft Beer Shop & Kitchen in Shorewood and stay for beer and food.

JOINING US IS FREE AND EASY:

  1. Arrive at Foxhole Craft Beer Shop & Kitchen on Wednesdays at 6pm
  2. Sign in with Ryan (just ask!)
  3. Group pic and run start at 6:30 sharp!
  4. Follow the route to the Oak Leaf Trail and back
  5. Finish at Foxhole Craft Beer Shop & Kitchen
  6. All night happy hour at the AFTERPARTY!

RUNNING ROUTE

We begin at Foxhole Craft Beer Shop & Kitchen (4144 N Oakland Ave). Run west on the sidewalk of Olive to N Wilson Dr. Cross at the crosswalk on Wilson and hop on the Oak Leaf Trail. You may run in either direction on Oak Leaf and return on Olive to Oakland, than back to Foxhole.

If you go north until Hampton, the route is approximately 5k. If you go south to Locust, the route is about 4 miles.
